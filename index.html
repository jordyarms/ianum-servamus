The Administration of Wonder

The Administration of Wonder is a secretive yet benevolent organization that exists at the intersection of imagination, science, ○○●○●○●● and magic.

Its mission is to cultivate and protect the sense of wonder across all worlds—whether in the hearts of the playful, the dreams of the hopeful, or the enterprise of the curious.

As stewards of potential, the Administration strives to tilt the balance of possibility toward awe—and away from calamity.

For wonder is not without its weight. Within every spark of curiosity lies the promise of fire, and every door, once opened, is open on both sides.
